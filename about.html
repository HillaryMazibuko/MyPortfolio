I am a GIS graduate from Malawi University of Science and Technology with practical experience in GIS analysis, land surveying, baseline mapping, and web development.

My field experience includes baseline mapping data collection with ADDA Emergency Unit in Salima and land survey fieldwork in Golomoti, Mangochi, where I participated in boundary surveys, parcel measurements, and spatial data validation.

In addition to geospatial work, I develop responsive websites and GIS-ready web platforms using modern web technologies. I am passionate about combining spatial data, field evidence, and software development to deliver data-driven solutions for planning and development.
